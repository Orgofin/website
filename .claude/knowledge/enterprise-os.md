## The Core Line

> "Just as Windows became the OS for personal computers and iOS became the OS for mobile, Orgofin is becoming the Operating System for companies — the layer through which every business process, every decision, and every employee interaction flows."

## The Evolution Roadmap (per the source PDF, §11)

| Horizon                       | Timeline     | Milestone                                                                                | Revenue Target |
| ----------------------------- | ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| H1: HRMS Wedge                | 0–12 months  | Best HRMS in India; 500+ customers; AI-native payroll-to-retire                          | $2–5M ARR      |
| H2: Full-Stack SMB OS         | 12–36 months | HRMS + Finance + CRM + Desk + Workspace live; 3,000+ customers; UK/US launched           | $20–40M ARR    |
| H3: AGaaS Platform            | 36–60 months | Agent Marketplace live; 10,000+ customers; AGaaS >30% of revenue                         | $100–200M ARR  |
| H4: Company Brain as Platform | 5–8 years    | Third-party developers build on Orgofin's Company Brain API; ecosystem of 10,000+ agents | $500M+ ARR     |
| H5: Enterprise OS             | 8–12 years   | Orgofin is the default OS for mid-market companies globally; IPO or strategic exit       | $1B+ ARR       |

## The 40+ Module / 8-Suite Structure

The full platform, at maturity, spans 8 functional suites: HR & People (Orgofin HRMS), Finance & Operations (Orgofin Books), Sales & Revenue (Orgofin CRM), Support & CX (Orgofin CX), Collaboration & Productivity (Orgofin Workspace), IT & Security (Orgofin IT Ops), Data/AI/Automation (Orgofin Intelligence), Compliance & Admin (Orgofin Governance). See `docs/product/copy.md` §3 for the current Available-Now/Roadmap status of each.

## The Enduring Promise (closing line, source PDF p.27)

> "We are not building another SaaS tool. We are building the last platform a company will ever need — a brain that grows smarter with every employee hired, every invoice raised, every customer served, and every decision made. The fragmented SaaS era is ending. The Company Brain era is beginning."
