## One-Line Vision

Orgofin is the unified Company Brain and Agent-as-a-Service (AGaaS) platform that replaces the fragmented SaaS stack with a single intelligence layer — where AI agents operate as digital employees across every business function.

## Elevator Pitch

Every company today runs on 20–80 disconnected SaaS tools — Tally for accounting, Zoho for CRM, Slack for messaging, Jira for projects, Keka for HR. None of them talk to each other. Data is duplicated, decisions are slow, and AI cannot understand the full business context.

Orgofin builds a unified Company Brain — a single platform where every employee, process, workflow, document, transaction, and decision lives in one interconnected graph — and deploys AI agents on top of it that work autonomously across departments. It launches with Orgofin HRMS (India-first, hire-to-retire) and progressively expands into Finance, CRM, CX, Collaboration, and IT.

## Why Now

- AI has crossed the capability threshold for reliable multi-step business automation.
- India's digitisation wave (GST mandates, DPDP Act, e-invoicing, UPI rails) is forcing 60M+ SMEs to replace legacy tools.
- Global SMB SaaS fatigue: the average mid-market company spends $3,500/employee/year on fragmented SaaS.
- Foundation models (GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini) made context-aware agents affordable for the first time.
- Post-Zoho, no India-born player has credibly attempted a full-stack, agent-native enterprise OS.

## The Problem (Fragmentation)

A typical Indian SME with 200 employees uses 7+ vendors and 15+ tools, spending $52K–$220K/year (₹1.5Cr+) with zero unified context. The ten core failures named in the source material: siloed data, no organisational memory after attrition, fragmented per-tool AI, brittle manual integrations, duplicate data entry, a 30–40% context-switching tax, compliance gaps with no single audit trail, slow cross-system decisions, lock-in without network effects, and prohibitive total cost.

## The Six Moats

1. **Organisational Memory** — compounds per customer; a switcher's replacement starts from zero.
2. **Data Network Effects** — anonymised cross-customer benchmarks (payroll, GST compliance, attrition) improve agents over time.
3. **India Compliance Depth** — IRP e-invoicing API, PF/ESI challans, Form 16/24Q, Aadhaar e-KYC, DPDP — months of regulatory engineering.
4. **Ecosystem Lock-in Without Hostility** — switching cost rises through value, not contracts.
5. **Agent Learning Loops** — agents improve from every task executed.
6. **CA/CS Partner Network** — 350,000 Chartered Accountants in India as a potential distribution channel.

## Business Model

Revenue streams: SaaS subscriptions, AGaaS usage billing (per-agent-task), Agent Marketplace (30% revenue share), Professional Services, Compliance & Data Services, Partner Channel revenue share.

Pricing tiers: Starter $99–199/org/mo (100 agent tasks) · Growth $5–8/user/mo (500 tasks) · Business $10–15/user/mo (2,000 tasks) · Enterprise custom/₹15L+ ACV (unlimited) · AGaaS Add-on $0.05–0.10/task.
