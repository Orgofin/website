## 0. Voice & Tone (quick reference)

Flagged as missing in [`prd.md`](./prd.md) §20.4 — a short working version so copy across pages doesn't drift:

- **Confident, not hype-y.** State facts plainly ("A 200-person company spends ₹1.5Cr/year on fragmented software") rather than adjectives ("incredible," "revolutionary").
- **Short sentences. Then room to breathe.** Cinematic pacing — one idea per line, not paragraphs of persuasion.
- **India-proud, globally ambitious.** Compliance depth is stated as an advantage, never apologized for or buried.
- **Plain English before jargon.** "Company Brain" and "AGaaS" always get a one-sentence plain-language translation before or alongside the term — see Company Brain hero copy below.
- **Show, don't sell.** Prefer the CEO Intelligence Agent vignette style (concrete, specific, almost boring in its detail) over adjectives about "the future of work."
