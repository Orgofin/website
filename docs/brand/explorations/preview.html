Orgofin — Logo Explorations

15 original directions, built to feel like a next-generation fintech + AI platform — simple, geometric, recognizable at any size. Each card shows the logo lockup, monochrome, app icon, and 32/16 px favicons, on light and dark. No dollar signs, coins, or generic sparkles. Colors (indigo→violet) and the Geist wordmark are placeholders. Rationale for every concept is in logo-explorations.md. SVGs are inlined as data URIs so this file renders correctly even when opened directly from disk.
